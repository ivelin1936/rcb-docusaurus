# Garage

The Garage section lets members manage their personal vehicle collection, track maintenance, check vehicle legality status via Bulgarian government APIs, and share their cars publicly with the community.

- [Car Management](./car-management) — Add, edit, and delete your cars
- [Maintenance Alerts](./maintenance-alerts) — Track maintenance reminders
- [Vehicle Checks (MOT, Vignette, Road Tax)](./vehicle-checks) — Check vehicle legality status
- [Public Car Profiles & Ratings](./car-ratings) — Community car profiles and star ratings
