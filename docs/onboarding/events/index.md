The events system is one of the core features of the RCB platform. Club events are managed end-to-end: from creation and applications through competition results and digital check-in.

- [Browsing Events](./browsing-events) — View upcoming and past events
- [Applying to Events](./applying-to-events) — Submit an application, request a car number
- [Event Check-in (Member QR)](./event-checkin) — Generate your digital check-in ticket
- [Event Results & Scoreboard](./event-results) — View competition results, podium, and rankings
- [Peer Badge Nominations](./peer-badge-nominations) — Nominate fellow members for recognition
